Eligible users will receive a special in-app reward: the "Starter Pack" containing 2 exclusive premium coloring worlds ("Wild Animals" and "Toy Box"). The reward is a digital in-app product. It has no cash value and cannot be transferred, exchanged, paid out in cash, or sold.

The delivery of the reward is completely automated. To claim your reward, you must meet the following criteria:

Once the app is launched and connects to the Google Play Store, the reward will automatically be unlocked and added to your in-app content. No manual claiming, email registration, or promo code is required.
