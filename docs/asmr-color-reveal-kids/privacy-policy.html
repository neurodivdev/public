Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ASMR Color Reveal Kids ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

The App collects minimal data to provide you with a great experience:

  • Game Progress: Your coloring progress, completed images, and earned coins are stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: Settings like sound and haptic feedback preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is handled by Google Play. We only store whether you have unlocked premium features.

Data Storage

All your game data and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Google Play Billing: Processes in-app purchases securely. Google handles all payment information.

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their data collection practices.

Children's Privacy

ASMR Color Reveal Kids is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) guidelines.

Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Clear the App's data through your device settings to remove all locally stored information.
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements in your device's privacy settings.
  • Uninstall the App at any time to remove all associated data from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
